    3 Facts About Me!

    I'd like to start this blog with three facts about me. The first fact is that I Love horror movies! I don't have a particular favourite and I find new ones all the time but I am a fan of classics such as Nightmare on Elm Street and Halloween! I will always be up for watching a horror movie. Even the really scary ones!

    The second fact about me is that I love the colours blue and grey! They are the colours of my room and they are very calming. I would also like to wear grey and blue even though I don't usually wear them but I think they would suit me!

    The third fact about me is that I am very sporty! I played many sports when I was younger such as.... tennis, swmming, basketball, netball, table tennis, cross country, football, jiu jitsu... and many more!

    Bonus fact: I love dogs! They are very cute and very friendly. I also do like cats.

    My Favourite Foods

    There are many favourite foods that I have and I would like to share them. The first favourite food that I enjoy is pizza! Most people enjoy pizza and so do I. There are many different toppings you can add to personalise your pizza and the tomato base is so yummy!

    The second food I would like to list is another Italian classic which is bruschetta. Even though this is classed as a side or a starter, it is a fairly simple dish and tastes amazing! As Adam from Inspired Taste likes to say:

    "When I see fresh tomatoes, my mind immediately turns to bruschetta." - Adam from Inspired Taste
    Inspired Taste

    My absolute favourite food of all time has got to be sushi. You either love it or hate it but so far I have never had a single bad experience with sushi. The texture of rice with the seaweed. Not to mention it can fairly good for your health, particularly the gut.

    My Hobbies

    I have many hobbies that I would like to share with you! Currently I play badminton with my friends and go swimming from time to time. I enjoy keeping myself fit and healthy, eating all the right foods and exercising is important to me. Although I do not think I would be able to master as many sports as I did when I was younger :(

    Since I was in secondary school I have been a fan of origiami and I usually practice when I am home in my room. I find this hobby to be very calming and it also helps me to clear my mind if I am feeling stressed. There are many useful origami projects I have made in my past and continue to make. Some of these include: storage boxes, lamps, bowls and pots.

    Another hobby I would like to mention is that I like to paint my nails every week! I have over 18 bottles of nail polish (as of 2024) and I will continue to grow that number!
